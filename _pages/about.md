Hello! I'm Linchao Pan, a second-year Master's student at [the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering](https://csse.szu.edu.cn/en), [Shenzhen University](https://en.szu.edu.cn/), under the supervision of [Prof. Can Gao](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=yyi9jUIAAAAJ). I received the Bachelor's degree in software engineering from [the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering](https://csse.szu.edu.cn/en), [Shenzhen University](https://en.szu.edu.cn/), in 2023. My major research mainly focuses on learning with noisy labels and semi-supervised learning.
